Six ways MediHelp responds

Emergency SOS

One tap shares your live GPS location with the nearest volunteer team and notifies your emergency contact.

AI Health Assistant

Ask about symptoms, first aid, or CPR steps and get guidance drawn from a 1,000+ question medical knowledge base.

Doctor Directory

Search Salem specialists by department, hospital, or distance from your current location.

Blood Donor Network

Filter donors by blood group and district, then call directly when every minute counts.

Volunteer Teams

Fire, medical, and rescue groups are matched automatically based on the emergency you report.

Emergency Shelters

Locate the nearest school, college, or community hall designated as a disaster shelter.
In an emergency

What happens after you press SOS

Step 1

Confirm & classify

A quick warning screen confirms the alert and captures the emergency type.

Step 2

Locate

Your live GPS coordinates and timestamp are captured automatically.

Step 3

Notify

The nearest matching volunteer team, your emergency contact, and the admin monitoring desk are alerted in real time.

Step 4

Track

A live tracking screen keeps you and responders updated until the situation is resolved.
